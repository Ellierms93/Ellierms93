A peaceful countryside escape

The Camp Inn is a small, family-friendly campsite and smallholding created for people who enjoy fresh air, open space and a slower pace of life. We are not a large commercial holiday park. Our aim is to offer a relaxed and welcoming place where guests can camp, unwind, spend time together and enjoy the natural surroundings.

Keeping everyone safe

We want every guest to enjoy a peaceful, safe and family-friendly stay. Our site rules are in place to protect visitors, children, animals and the countryside around us.

  • Please check in on arrival.
  • Quiet hours are from 10pm to 8am.
  • Please drive slowly — children and animals on site.
  • Children must be supervised at all times.
  • Dogs must be kept on leads and cleaned up after.
  • Please use bins provided and leave your pitch tidy.
