Our concept

The vision for The Camp Inn

The Camp Inn is being planned as a small, welcoming campsite and smallholding with a natural rural feel. The aim is to create somewhere calm, friendly and well-presented — not a busy commercial holiday park, but a place where guests can slow down and enjoy simple countryside living.

The images on this website are concept visuals showing the style, atmosphere and direction we are working towards as the site develops.

Camping

Planned campsite experience

The campsite is being planned as a peaceful and family-friendly place to stay, with a focus on calm surroundings, safe access, clear site rules and a welcoming check-in experience.

Planned features

  • Grass camping pitches
  • Family-friendly atmosphere
  • Dogs welcome with clear rules
  • Reception/check-in point
  • Rustic entrance signage
  • Quiet hours and safety information

Smallholding

Animals, fresh air and family memories

The smallholding is part of what will make The Camp Inn feel special. The vision is for guests to enjoy the charm of animals and rural life, while keeping safety, hygiene and animal welfare at the heart of the experience.

  • Clear animal feeding rules
  • Handwashing stations
  • Separated livestock areas
  • Family-friendly farm feel
  • Calm, supervised animal interaction

Facilities vision

The site is being designed to feel practical, clean, rustic and welcoming — with facilities that suit a countryside campsite while still feeling thoughtful and well-branded.

Families

Space to play safely

The playground vision is natural, simple and family-friendly, with clear rules and a safe bark area so children can play while families enjoy the relaxed campsite setting.

As the site develops, safety, supervision and suitable signage will be built into the layout from the beginning.

What happens next?

The Camp Inn is currently in the planning and preparation stage. We are working towards creating a safe, welcoming and beautiful rural site.

1

Preparing the land

Planning the entrance, access, pitch areas, fencing, services and overall site layout.

2

Building facilities

Creating practical toilet, shower, handwashing and guest facilities in the same rustic style.

3

Smallholding setup

Creating safe animal areas, clear guest boundaries, feeding rules and handwashing points.

4

Opening soon

Once ready, we will share booking details, opening dates and updates for visitors.
